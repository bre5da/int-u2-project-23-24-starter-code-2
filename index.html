Food insecurity refers to the lack of consistent access to enough food for an active, healthy life. It can result from various factors such as poverty, unemployment, or inadequate infrastructure. Food insecuirty affaects millions worldwide, leading to malnurtrition, health issues, and perpetuating cycles of poverty.
